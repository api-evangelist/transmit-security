Transmit Security introduces Transaction Monitoring for its Detection and Response Services, targeting fraud that only becomes visible when an attacker tries to move money, add a payee, or change account details. The post argues fraud prevention is a race against timing, and that catching risk at the moment of the transaction gives organizations a better chance to protect customers and prevent loss.
